Freelance video editor platform, Valoso, brings you “One Shot”, the online video animation contest for VFX enthusiasts.

The video tech company has uploaded video content for you to make your masterpiece. Choose 1 footage from 5 different stock footage supplied and apply your skill. Composite it the way you like. Integrate 3d assets, live action, simulation, effects, anything goes. Let your imagination take control and your vision come alive. The crazier the better but it has to look realistic. Realism is key here.
