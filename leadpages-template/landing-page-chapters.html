The Ultimate Guide to Video Marketing

What is video marketing?

Video marketing is the incorporation of videos into a marketing strategy. Marketing videos function similarly to blogging, image ads and other forms of marketing, with the intent of promoting a company, business or service. Video marketing, however, offers more promotional flexibility than the other mediums by giving you the opportunity to use real people in your promotion and incorporate visually appealing elements like animation or motion graphics.

There is no limit to the places whereonline video marketing can be used, because the online places where your video is promoted havethe video embed option. Whether it individual websites, social media, or email blasts, marketing videos can create brand awareness or expand the knowledge of those already familiar with your product.

Why is video marketing so important today?

As stated above, video is an incredibly powerful tool, accessible through YouTube, Facebook, Twitter, and other social media platforms. According to Facebook, 500 million of its users watch videos on the site every day. Most of these videos come from YouTube, the central hub of online video marketing. Even Instagram has jumped on the bandwagon, recently extending its video recording limit to 60 seconds in order to capitalize on the popularity of video sharing.

Naturally, marketing tactics have evolved with this shift in the way the public discovers information. Video marketing has risen alongside the popularity of these websites. Statistics show that Internet users are much more likely to click a play button than read text about a product.

Merely making a video is not enough, however. Be careful not to disregard video marketing strategy. Videos are most effective when they capitalize on trends and take into account a product’s target audience among other things. For instance, the shorter a video is, the more likely it is for viewers to watch to the end. Video marketing services like Valoso help businesses by providing expert video marketing tips to small businesses so they can better develop their own video marketing strategy.

Video Statistics—wow!

Did you know?

Vidyard reported that 70 percent of marketers say video converts better than other mediums. A report from online shoe and clothing company, Zappos, also emphasized the worth of having videos on websites, reporting that product pages with video convert up to 30% more users than pages without video.

According to a study by ComStats, online video reaches 84.5 percent of the U.S. audience. In a recently published visual networking forecast, Cisco reported that globally, IP video traffic will account for 82 percent of all consumer Internet traffic by 2020. That is a 70 percent rise from 2015. Another study from Forrester Research found that web pages that feature videos are 50 times more likely to appear on the first page of Google than websites without video. Social and behavioral research from the University of Twente indicated that 91% of Google searchers don’t even look past the first page of Google.

There are also ways of properly marketing videos so they get the attention they deserve. One of the most effective ways to do this is through your video SEO, or using a catchy, commonly searched title. According to Search Engine Journal, 93% of online experiences begin with a search engine.
